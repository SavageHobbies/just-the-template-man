Grading Scale

We grade our collectibles on a scale of 1 - 10. For Funko Pops, we grade the condition of the outside box.

  • 10: Mint – Perfect.
  • 9: Almost Mint – Very minor imperfections, usually only visible under close inspection.
  • 8: Some Minor Box Damage – Small dents/creases.
  • 7: Moderate Box Damage – Noticeable damage.
  • 6: Box Damage – Significant damage.
  • 5: Major Box Damage, Funko Ok.

  • 3: Out of Box, Excellent.
  • 2: Out of Box, Moderate Wear.
  • 1: Out of Box, Played With - Obvious signs of use.

Please note that our Funko Pop grading scale is subjective and may vary based on individual perspectives. We make every effort to disclose any flaws or defects that we identify, but we may not always catch every imperfection. The appearance of flaws can also be affected by lighting conditions and other environmental factors. Please carefully review the photos provided to assess the condition for yourself.
