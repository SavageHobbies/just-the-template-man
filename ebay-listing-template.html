Our Grading Scale

We grade our collectibles on a scale of 1-10. For items with packaging, we grade the condition of the outside packaging.

  • 10: Mint – Perfect condition.
  • 9: Near Mint – Very minor imperfections, usually only visible under close inspection.
  • 8: Minor Damage – Small dents/creases that don't affect overall appeal.
  • 7: Moderate Damage – Noticeable damage but still displayable.
  • 6: Significant Damage – Obvious damage affecting appearance.
  • 5: Major Damage – Substantial damage, item still intact.
  • 4: Heavily Damaged – May include removed stickers or tears.
  • 3: Out of Package, Excellent – No packaging, item in great condition.
  • 2: Out of Package, Good – No packaging, moderate wear.
  • 1: Poor Condition – Obvious signs of heavy use or damage.
Grading Disclaimer:
Our grading scale is subjective and may vary based on individual perspectives. We strive for accuracy and consistency, but grading is not an exact science. We tend to grade conservatively and disclose all visible flaws. Please review photos carefully, as lighting and angles may affect appearance. Contact us for additional photos if needed.
